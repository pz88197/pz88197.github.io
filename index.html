Dancing is also something I have to mention because it has been playing a vital role in my life. When I was little, I was such a chubby girl that nobody except my parents believed I could dance well among all the other slim girls. However, as soon as I started dancing, I became the best dancer in people's eyes. Believe it or not, I got this talent inside of me. My mom always tells me that I look like an entirely different person when I dance; I am joyful and confident. Dancing sets me free from all the laughing I received for my short and fat body.
